# Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how users can delete their account and personal data from the app, in accordance with Google Playâ€™s User Data and Account Deletion requirements.

---

## How to Request Account Deletion

Users can delete their account directly from within the app by following these steps:

1. Open the app and sign in to your account.
2. Navigate to the **Profile** screen.
3. Select **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm your request when prompted.

Once confirmed, the deletion request is made. An admin will confirm the request and ensure all outstanding orders are filfilled then delete the account.

---

## What Data Is Deleted

When an account deletion request is completed:

- The **user profile** is permanently deleted.
- All **personal data** associated with the user is removed.
- This includes:
  - Name
  - Email address
  - Phone number
  - Address
  - Chat messages
  - Order-related personal data

---

## What Data Is Anonymized

For operational and historical consistency:

- Records that reference the deleted user (such as orders or logs) will have the **user identifier set to `null`**.
- These records will no longer be associated with any identifiable user.
- No personal data remains linked to the deleted account.

---

## Retention and Processing Time

- Account deletion is initiated immediately after user confirmation.
- Data removal and anonymization are completed automatically as part of the deletion process.
- No personal data is retained after deletion, except where required by law.

---

## Important Notes

- Account deletion is **permanent** and **cannot be undone**.
- Once deleted, the account cannot be recovered.
- Users must create a new account to use the Service again.
