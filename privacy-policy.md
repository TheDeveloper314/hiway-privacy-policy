## Information Collection and Use

To provide and improve our Service, we collect certain personally identifiable information. The information we collect may include:

- **Name**
- **Email address**
- **Phone number**
- **Address**
- **Order details** (such as product links, quantities, size, color, weight)
- **Messages exchanged through in-app chat**

This information is used strictly to:

- Create and manage user accounts
- Process and manage orders
- Communicate with users
- Provide customer support
- Improve app functionality and user experience

We do not sell or share your personal information for advertising purposes.
